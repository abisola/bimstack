Renew your medical licence

You can renew online from your phone, or book a short appointment at the Medical Council to do it in person with our help.

Both paths take you to the same place – a current annual licence and a certificate from the Council.

Not sure which?

Start online – you can stop and book an appointment any time if it gets stuck.

Online renewal

You'll need your Trident ID number, your CPD record, and a card for the BBD 200 fee. Most doctors finish in 10 minutes.

You can pause any time

If you get stuck, tap "Get help instead" and we'll book you an appointment. Anything you've already entered will be there when the Council sees you.

Assumptions to verify

This variant deliberately presents both paths with equal weight to test whether assisted-digital can be first-class without being patronising.

About the user

  • Verify with users Doctors who choose assisted-digital don't feel relegated or judged. (Standard 3, Standard 4)
  • Verify with users The two paths are read as equivalent, not as "easy path" vs "the hard one if you can't cope".
  • Verify with users Older doctors and doctors with poor smartphone access find the booked-appointment route easy to discover.
  • Verify with users "Get help instead" at every online step is reassuring rather than confusing.

About the Council's capacity

  • Verify with MDA The Council can offer 30-minute booked slots at scale, including during the renewal peak. (Standard 8 – sustainability)
  • Verify with MDA Council officers are happy to be the assisted-digital channel, with the right training. (Standard 2)
  • Verify with MDA A booking system can be wired up to the Council's existing rota and physical space.
  • Verify with data What share of doctors today need any kind of in-person help with the renewal? Sets the demand for the assisted slots.

About the platform

  • Verify with MIST An appointment-booking pattern exists in the design system, or needs to be added. (Standard 9 – propose back)
  • Verify with MIST SMS notifications via a shared GovTech channel are available for reminders.
  • Known gap The online-to-assisted hand-off ("save what I've entered, the Council can see it") needs a real session-state mechanism. (Standard 7)

About policy

  • Verify with policy Booking a renewal slot doesn't create a regulatory expectation that the appointment will succeed regardless of evidence. (Council retains regulatory discretion.)
  • Verify with policy A doctor's data carried from online into an assisted appointment is held legally for that purpose. (Standard 11)
