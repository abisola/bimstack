Renew your medical licence

Use this service to renew your annual practising licence with the Medical Council of Barbados.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • Your Trident ID number, or your National Registration Number
  • Your CPD record for the last year (PDF or photo)
  • A debit or credit card to pay the BBD 200 renewal fee

How long it takes

Most doctors finish this in about 10 minutes. You can save your work and come back later.

When you'll get your certificate

We'll send your new certificate to your registered email within 5 working days.

If you can't renew online

Call the Medical Council on [FAKE NUMBER] 246-555-0100 and someone will help you. We can also book an appointment at the Council for you.

What's your Trident ID?

This is the 12-digit number on your Trident ID card, or your National Registration Number (NRN).

We'll look up your name, contact details, and current registration. You won't have to type them in.

Assumptions to verify

Every claim this prototype makes that's not yet evidence-led. Tested in user research and stakeholder review.

About the user

  • Verify with users Doctors will trust a Trident ID lookup to fetch their licence details. (Standard 7, Standard 11)
  • Verify with users A 10-minute end-to-end flow on a phone is realistic in a doctor's working day. (Standard 5)
  • Verify with users "Save and come back later" reduces abandonment for doctors interrupted by clinical work. (Standard 5)
  • Verify with users Plain-language labels ("Where do you practise?" not "Practice modality classification") feel respectful rather than patronising. (Standard 4)

About the data

  • Verify with MDA The Medical Council can accept CPD evidence as a single uploaded file (PDF or photo). (Standard 5, Standard 7)
  • Verify with MDA 40 hours is the correct minimum CPD requirement for full registration renewal. (Standard 4 – we shouldn't ask if we don't know the rule)
  • Verify with MDA Renewal fee is BBD 200 across all categories of registration.
  • Verify with data Five working days is a realistic SLA for the certificate. Today's mean is unknown.

About the platform

  • Verify with MIST Trident ID returns the data fields shown on the Confirm page (name, NRN, parish, email, phone). (Standard 7)
  • Verify with MIST The Trident ID lookup returns medical-licence registration data, or this requires a separate Medical Council register call.
  • Verify with MIST The shared payment gateway supports BBD 200 single transactions, with a webhook back to this service on success.
  • Known gap Specialist CPD (e.g. RCS, RCP requirements) isn't yet represented in the upload step.

About policy

  • Verify with policy Doctors can legally complete the renewal entirely digitally – no wet signature required. (Medical Profession Act review needed.)
  • Verify with policy Personal data flow between Trident ID, Medical Council, and the payment gateway is permitted under the Data Protection Act for this purpose. (Standard 11)
