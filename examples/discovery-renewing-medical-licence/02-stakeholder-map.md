## Risks visible at week 0

- Multiple regulatory bodies may overlap (Medical Council + CAAM-HP + Ministry). A turf question may surface and slow the discovery. `[TO WATCH]`
- Doctors are time-poor; recruitment for research will be harder than for a typical citizen service. The delivery manager should budget for paid recruitment via BAMP or hospital HR. `[TO PLAN]`
- A small cohort with strong professional representation means anything we propose will be socialised quickly – a feature for working in the open (Standard 9), but worth being aware of.
